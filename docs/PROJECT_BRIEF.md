# PROJECT_BRIEF.md — Bharat Bhraman Tour & Travels

## Client & goal

Tour and travel agency specializing in Ladakh + Himachal circuits. Goal of the site: generate qualified enquiries (form + WhatsApp) for packages, rank organically for Ladakh/Himachal package and guide keywords, and look like a premium specialist rather than a listings portal.

## Target audience

1. **Domestic Indian travelers** (primary): couples/honeymooners, friend groups, bikers, families — planning Leh-Ladakh trips May–October
2. **NRI / international travelers** wanting a trusted local operator
3. **Corporate/group offsites** (secondary)

## Funnel

Traffic (SEO guides + social + ads later) → package/destination pages → **enquiry form or WhatsApp** → human follow-up → booking offline. No payments on site (phase 2 possibility: Razorpay payment links sent on WhatsApp — no site work needed).

## Destination universe (v1 content targets)

**Ladakh:** Leh (local sightseeing: Shanti Stupa, Leh Palace, Hall of Fame, Magnetic Hill, Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, Sangam), Nubra Valley (Diskit, Hunder, Khardung La), Turtuk & Thang (last villages), Pangong Tso, Tso Moriri, Hanle (dark sky reserve), Umling La, Kargil & Drass, Lamayuru, Alchi & Likir, Sham Valley
**Himachal:** Manali (+ Atal Tunnel, Solang, Sissu), Bir Billing (paragliding), (expandable later: Spiti, Kasol, Tirthan)

## Package set v1 (seed — client to confirm pricing)

## Guide hub v1 (SEO moat)

- Inner Line Permit for Ladakh: complete guide (who needs it, how, cost)
- Acclimatization in Ladakh: how to not ruin your trip (AMS, itinerary pacing, Diamox facts — medical accuracy required, advise consulting a doctor)
- Best time to visit Ladakh: month-by-month matrix
- Manali–Leh vs Srinagar–Leh: which road and why
- Pangong vs Tso Moriri vs Hanle: choosing your lakes and skies
- Turtuk & Thang: visiting India's last villages respectfully
- What actually works in Ladakh: ATMs, fuel, mobile network (Ladakh is postpaid-only for outside SIMs — verify current rules)
- Bir Billing paragliding: first-timer's guide

## Success criteria

- Lighthouse mobile ≥ 90 performance, 100 SEO/accessibility/best-practices targets
- Every commercial page reachable ≤ 2 clicks from home
- Enquiry form completion < 60 seconds; WhatsApp CTA visible on every screen
- Editors can add a package end-to-end in Sanity Studio with zero dev help

## Out of scope v1

Payments, user accounts, live availability, multi-language (Hindi phase 2), blog comments, third-party review widgets (embed later).
